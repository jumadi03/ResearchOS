## Authority and scope

PMC-000 is the canonical single orientation point and documentation index for
ResearchOS. It directs readers to governing sources and distinguishes canonical
documents, operational evidence, working direction, candidates, roadmaps, and
historical material.

PMC-000 does not replace DOC-000, DOC-001, SGF-000, SGF-020, the Canonical
Document Register, the production registry, or exact dated acceptance reports.
It does not create canonical status for DKA, DEC, RCA, CFIC, SGF-ARCH, SOK, REA,
FMA, or any other non-admitted artifact merely by naming it.

Ratification does not accept scientific evidence, establish scientific truth,
ratify ontology content, prove implementation maturity, or establish general
production fitness. Time-sensitive operational statements remain bounded by
their cited dates and evidence.

This is a project-owner governance decision. ResearchOS has no separately
demonstrated independent human governance reviewer, so this decision is not
independent peer review or scientific validation.
