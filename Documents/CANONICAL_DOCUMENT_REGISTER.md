## Inventory conclusion

No other ResearchOS document currently has the complete combination of an
explicit human ratification decision, a Published Canonical Edition
representation, a matching frozen hash, and a canonical admission record.

repository files that use the word "canonical" descriptively are not admitted
by this register.

## Register maintenance

An addition, correction, withdrawal, or supersession must be supported by an
immutable decision record and verified representation hash. Historical entries
must not be silently removed.
