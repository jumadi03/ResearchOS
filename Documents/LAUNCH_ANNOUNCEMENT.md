# ResearchOS v0.4.0 Launch Announcement

ResearchOS `v0.4.0` is the first public, reproducible release of the project.
The copy below is ready to adapt for a project page, community forum, or social
post. Preserve the experimental-status and human-review statements when
shortening it.

## English announcement

ResearchOS v0.4.0 is now publicly available.

ResearchOS is an experimental, provenance-first platform for scientific
knowledge workflows. It connects literature discovery, document integrity,
evidence review, knowledge graphs, theory synthesis, validation, publication,
and deterministic software-architecture governance without allowing AI output
to silently become accepted scientific evidence.

The first public release includes:

- a secure, one-command local bootstrap;
- PostgreSQL and MinIO canonical storage with versioned migrations;
- role-separated discovery, review, indexing, and administration;
- resilient workers, monitoring, verified backups, and recovery checks;
- a browser workspace for objects, provenance, review queues, and graphs;
- 149 passing regression tests and six required GitHub quality gates; and
- reproducible release artifacts with checksums, an SBOM, provenance metadata,
  and a GitHub build attestation.

ResearchOS remains experimental. Its interfaces may change, AI-generated
material is advisory, and outputs do not replace scientific peer review,
professional judgment, or regulatory validation.

## Pengumuman bahasa Indonesia

ResearchOS v0.4.0 kini tersedia untuk publik.

ResearchOS adalah platform eksperimental yang mengutamakan provenance untuk
workflow pengetahuan ilmiah. Platform ini menghubungkan discovery literatur,
integritas dokumen, review evidence, knowledge graph, sintesis teori, validasi,
publikasi, dan tata kelola arsitektur perangkat lunak yang deterministik. Hasil
AI tidak dapat secara diam-diam berubah menjadi evidence ilmiah yang diterima.

Rilis publik pertama ini mencakup:

- bootstrap lokal aman dengan satu perintah;
- penyimpanan kanonik PostgreSQL dan MinIO dengan migrasi berversi;
- pemisahan peran untuk discovery, review, indexing, dan administrasi;
- worker yang resilien, monitoring, backup terverifikasi, dan pemeriksaan
  pemulihan;
- workspace browser untuk object, provenance, antrean review, dan graph;
- 149 pengujian regresi yang lulus dan enam quality gate wajib GitHub; serta
- artefak rilis reproducible dengan checksum, SBOM, metadata provenance, dan
  build attestation GitHub.

ResearchOS masih bersifat eksperimental. Antarmukanya dapat berubah, materi
yang dihasilkan AI hanya bersifat advisory, dan keluarannya tidak menggantikan
peer review ilmiah, penilaian profesional, atau validasi regulasi.
