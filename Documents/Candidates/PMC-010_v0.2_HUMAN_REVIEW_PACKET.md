## Review history

The project owner supplied an external PMC-010 v0.1 draft as a replacement for
the previously rejected general Orientation Guide. Review found that the
replacement correctly declared an informative scope but required revision
before repository admission because:

1. its onboarding order did not begin with root `AGENTS.md` and
   `.agents/AGENTS.md`;

   distinctions;
3. repository references lacked exact paths and hyperlinks;
4. non-canonical status and identifier effect required clearer wording; and
5. pagination, checklist markers, and source-table layout required correction.

PMC-010 v0.2 resolved those findings. The exact candidate was then rendered
through Microsoft Word and all five pages were inspected.
