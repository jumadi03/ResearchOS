# SGF Canonicalization Sequence

DOC-000 v0.2 requires exact-version review, explicit human ratification,
immutable publication, complete provenance, and dependency-aware fail-closed
admission.

2. `SGF-020` — human authority and decision admission.
3. `SGF-030` — scientific ontology; depends on SGF-020.
4. `SGF-040` — research-object lifecycle; depends on SGF-020 and SGF-030.

canonical admission merely because they are described in SGF-000.

No item in this sequence becomes canonical through this planning record. Each
requires its own frozen candidate hash, review packet, explicit human decision,
Published Canonical Edition, visual and structural verification, and canonical
admission record.
