## 2. Decision

The ResearchOS project owner accepts the exact reviewed draft as bounded
working direction for continued Governance Authority work.

The accepted working direction is:

- authority and permission must remain explicitly separated;
- authority claims must remain scoped to their bounded context;
- runtime, review, repository, CI, release, deployment, recovery, and
  publication permissions must not be promoted into project-governance
  authority;
- project-owner working-direction evidence must not be promoted into formal
  ratification without an independently evidenced authority basis;
- authority provenance must remain attributable to an exact object, decision
  class, scope, actor, basis, effect, and time;
- authority laundering and circular authorization must remain prohibited;
- unresolved authority must remain explicit; and
- any later assignment of formal authority must be treated as a separate
  decision problem.
