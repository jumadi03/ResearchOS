## Authority and scope

DOC-001 is the canonical foundational charter of the Proyek Teori Ruang
Orientasi. It establishes the project's origin, purpose, permanent principles,
human commitments, scientific-truth boundary, memory discipline, community,
and intended legacy.

Ratification of DOC-001 does not ratify the scientific truth of a six-axis
orientation theory, define missing axes, or admit a theory into the scientific
knowledge graph. Those matters remain subject to evidence, independent human
review, and the applicable Scientific Governance Framework.

ResearchOS is recognized by DOC-001 as the scientific-memory and operational
environment that arose after the project to preserve its provenance and
governed development.
